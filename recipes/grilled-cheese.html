Grilled Cheese

Everyone loves a good grilled cheese. Here we are going to make the classic treat! It is super simple and only takes a few minutes.

As usual BE PREPARED and make sure you have everything ready in front of you.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Sliced American Cheese

Recipe

  1. Step 1: Place pan on the stove and start preheating to a medium or medium-high heat.
  2. Step 2: Butter your bread on one side. You will need 2 slices per sandwich.
  3. Step 3: Place one slice of bread butter side down in the preheated pan.
  4. Step 4: Place 2 slices of cheese on the bread in the pan and top with the other piece of bread making sure the butter side is facing up.
  5. Step 5: When bottom of sandwhich is browned to your liking, flip the sandwich and cook until the other side is browned as well.
  6. Step 6: Remove from pan and cut diagnolly.
    Note: For best cheese pull, let rest for about 30-60 seconds before cutting and then wiggle the pieces together a little.
  7. Step 7: Enjoy!
