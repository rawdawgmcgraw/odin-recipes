Fried Chicken

Perfect Fried Chicken with a Kick

You have found the perfect fried chicken recipe, look no further! I will be showing you everything you need to know about making this amazing chicken. Frying anything can be daunting but it is easier than you think. We will be using kitchen staples and basic cookware throughout the entire process so anyone can do it.

The importnt thing to remember is BE PREPARED!!

Make sure all your seasonings are ready, your chicken is thawed completely, and your oil is preheated in the pan so everything can run smoothly and efficiently.

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken drumsticks
  • 2 eggs (for egg wash)
  • 1/2 cup of flour
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp onion powder
  • 4 tsp smoked paprika
  • salt
  • pepper

Recipe

  1. Step 1: Using a dutch oven, or another large pot, fill the pot about half way with oil and begin heating the oil to around 375 degrees.
  2. Step 2: Lay out your chicken on a baking sheet or cutting board. Season genrerously with salt and pepper on all sides.
  3. Step 3: Mix together the flour and dry seasongings to create your dredge. Add salt and pepper to taste here.
  4. Step 4: Whisk your eggs in a bowl with a small splash of milk or water to make your egg wash.
  5. Step 5: Begin dredging your chicken. Take a piece and roll it in the seasoned flour, followed by the egg wash, then once more in the flour and into the hot oil. This creates a nice cruchy outer layer that is full of favor. Repeat with all of your chicken.
  6. Step 6: Cook your chicken until the thickest part of meet reaches an internal temperature of 165 degrees farhenheit.
    Note: If your chicken is not fully submerged in the oil, make sure to turn it in the pan occasionaly so it cooks evenly on all sides.
  7. Step 7: Cover a plate with paper towels and place cooked chicken on top to rest for a few minutes.
